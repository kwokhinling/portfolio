Tsugihagi Honbo is a game designer that created a TRPG(in a book called)


tsugihagi-honpo

When I first into the game, I really feel depressed with the world setting. The world ends in a nuclear war, and necromancer(the holder of mold technology) can arise the dead army with dead bodies. Instead of fighting against the necromancer, players are dolls created by necromancer for "fun". Torturing them by sending them to dangerous quest and observe how their mind broken in the apocalypse. I have been in other TRPG like Dungeon & Dragon and Call of Cthunen, but I have never been in such a TRPG that requires put all the feeling into it. I was impressed that Tsugihagi Honbo create a world like this. In the world like this, the only things you could rely on are your "sisters"(other player), you are encouraged to shares your feelings with others and you are rewarded from doing it. You will feel sad, guilty, hopeless, and terrified during campaign, but you'll always have someone next to you supporting you physically, verbally, and mentally. After years of playing other TRPG, I've already been tired of communicating with others and tried to do my own stuff. This game reminds me that the core and original aspiration of TRPG is players sit down to play-- connected.
